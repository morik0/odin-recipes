Lasagna Recipe



Description

This Easy Lasagna Recipe from The Cozy Cook features a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and plenty of mozzarella cheese. Perfect for make-ahead meals and freezer-friendly, it requires simple ingredients but promises rich flavor. The dish is assembled in a 9 x 13-inch casserole with three layers of lasagna noodles, cheese filling, and meat sauce, topped with mozzarella. It’s baked at 375°F for 45 minutes and served best with garlic bread. Enhance flavors with hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce, and use high-quality marinara sauce for the best results. Store leftovers for up to 3 days in the refrigerator or 3 months in the freezer.


Ingredients


  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Herbs

Steps


  1. Make the tomato sauce.
  2. Stack the lasagna sheets and tomato sauce.
  3. Finally put cheese on top.
  4. Bake for 50 min. in the oven.
