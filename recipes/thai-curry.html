Thai Curry



Description

The homemade curry paste gives this curry an authentic taste; the tempeh provides a large portion of protein and adds a nutty note to the curry. A spicy, flavorful dish â€“ easy to prepare and incredibly delicious.


Ingredients


  • Rice
  • Coconut Milk
  • Garlic
  • Vegetables
  • Curry Paste

Steps


  1. Cut the vegetables.
  2. Clean rice and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Fry the vegetables in a pan.
  4. Add coconut milk and let cook for 5 more minutes.
