Pastel de Nata



Description

Pastel de nata is a traditional Portuguese custard tart, featuring a flaky, buttery pastry crust filled with a rich, creamy custard. Often enjoyed warm, it's sprinkled with cinnamon or powdered sugar, offering a delightful blend of textures and flavors. This iconic treat is beloved for its delicate balance of sweetness and its crisp, caramelized top.


Ingredients


  • puff pastry
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pastry.
  2. Make the Custard.
  3. Assamble and Bake.
